## Project Purpose
The purpose of 'Musical You' is to provide Spotify users with long-term insights into their listening habits through interactive analytics dashboards and generate personalized playlists based on user behavior. Essentialy it's Spotify Wrapped but you don't have to wait until the end of the year to see your statistics. 

----
## Project Objectives
- Allow users to securely log in via Spotify OAuth  
- Import and store listening history for analytics  
- Display trends in top tracks, artists, and genres over time  
- Generate smart playlists tailored to user preferences (mood, genre, rediscovery)
